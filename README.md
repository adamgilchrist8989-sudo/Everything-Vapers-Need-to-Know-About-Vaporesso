<h2>Tips for Getting Started</h2>
<h3>First-Time Setup</h3>
<p>Charge your device fully before first use. Most Vaporesso products ship partially charged, but a full charge ensures proper battery calibration.</p>
<p>Prime your coil by letting e-liquid soak for 5-10 minutes before vaping. This prevents burnt hits and extends coil life.</p>
<p>Start with lower wattages or tighter airflow settings. You can always increase intensity, but harsh hits turn people off vaping completely.</p>
<h3>E-Liquid Selection</h3>
<p>Match your liquid to your device's capabilities. Pod systems work best with salt-based e-liquids or 50/50 freebase options.</p>
<p>Start with familiar flavors. Tobacco and menthol ease the transition from smoking. Branch out to fruits and desserts once you're comfortable.</p>
<p>Buy small bottles initially. Preferences change quickly when you're new to vaping.</p>
<h3>Common Beginner Mistakes</h3>
<p>Don't chain vape immediately. Give your device breaks between long sessions. Coils need time to re-saturate.</p>
<p>Don't ignore weird tastes. Burnt or off flavors mean something's wrong. Usually it's a dying coil or low e-liquid.</p>
<p>Don't forget to turn off your device. Accidental firing in pockets drains batteries and can damage coils.</p>
